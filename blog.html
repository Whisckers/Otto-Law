Otto graduated with a Bachelor of Laws from the University of Botswana in 2006 and won the Law Department Book Prize in 2002/2003. He represented Botswana in international moot court competitions, including the Phillip Jessup Moot in Washington, DC, and the All Africa Human Rights Moot in South Africa. Otto is the founding partner of the firm and worked at Makuyana Legal Practice from 2004, becoming a High Court attorney in 2006. He also completed Commercial Law training through the Law Society of Botswana and the International Senior Lawyers Project.

Mr. Dipatane holds a Bachelor of Laws from North West University (2006) and was admitted to the Botswana Bar in 2007. Mr. Dipatane is experienced in advising on complex legal matters and has drafted and negotiated commercial agreements with both local and international entities.
