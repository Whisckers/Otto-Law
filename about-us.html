OUR MISSION

We ensure the preparation of the legal ground of the fields in which our clients operate, addressing the legal disputes they experience in their daily lives. This removes obstacles and provides legal services to facilitate their activities. With preventive legal services, we identify legal risks that may arise against our clients before a dispute occurs and aim to minimize these risks. We establish all our relationships with the trust required by the legal profession. To strengthen this trust, we work with all our might to resolve legal issues as quickly and accurately as possible.

COMMERCIAL LITIGATION AND ALTERNATIVE DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Fundamental to our approach is being frank with our clients in assessing their risk, both in financial terms and business reputation and establishing the best possible solutions that are effective. We deal with and in high value, complex and significant cases, finding cost effective solutions to disputes and advising our clients on pre-emptive measures of averting disputes. This division is led by a Senior Partner who underwent extensive dispute resolution training at postgraduate level and is well suited to handle complex situations. The division’s experience is signified by the right of audience that we enjoy throughout the country’s tribunals and panel of arbitrators and mediators. We deal with disputes involving highly complex claims and technical commercial disputes and the Litigation Department has extensive experience in a vast range of commercial disputes, including:

  • Regulatory Litigation
  • Competition Litigation before the Competition Commission
  • Contract Disputes
  • Labour Law Litigation
  • Commercial contracts
  • Construction Litigation
  • Regulatory and Licensing
  • Insolvency Disputes

DEBT COLLECTION

We act for a wide variety of clients in connection with the collection of debts both large and small. We can advise our clients on the best method of securing their debts and have an excellent working relationship with local Deputy Sheriffs. We use a computerized debt recovery system that enables us to carry out this work speedily and effectively.

  • Secured Repossessions
  • Debt Recovery
  • Motor Finance Repossessions
  • Foreclosures

COMMERCIAL & CORPORATE LAW

Our Corporate and Commercial division provides practical, proactive legal support to clients across various industries. We focus on delivering clear, results-driven advice and work closely with clients and their advisors to meet business objectives. Our team is committed to helping businesses grow by handling complex legal matters with expertise and care. Key areas we cover include:

  • Purchase and sale of commercial and investment property
  • Commercial leasing
  • Property development
  • Company and business acquisitions, mergers and sales
  • Commercial contracts
  • Commercial funding and security work
  • Regulatory and Licensing
  • Drafting and reviewing Commercial Agreements

WILLS, TRUSTS, EXECUTRIES & TAXATION

We specialise in giving tax planning advice both in relation to making wills and in respect of asset protection such as setting up Trusts to suit individual needs. We can offer a full administration service for trusts, but also operate as attorneys or guardians for clients. Our main areas of operation include:

  • Setting up and the Administration of Trusts
  • Inheritance Tax Planning
  • Executory Administration
  • Advice on and Drafting Wills
